   Ameya's Blurbs
About me:

♥

My name is Ameya (uh-may-uh) or Lindsay, depending on the circumstances in which we met. Ameya is preferred. I'm going to Wright State University, majoring in History with a minor in International Studies. I eventually want to be a professor of South Asian studies.

♥

I'm super involved in school, I'm Public Relations Officer (and founding member) of Inter-Cultural Exchange which works closely with the Center of International Education in programming for the international students here and the American students who are interested in the world. I'm also in the fabulous Delta Zeta sorority, University Activities Board, Feminist Majority Leadership Alliance, Society for Creative Anachronism, Peacemakers, and probably a bunch of other things. I also work a little at a local college prep school where I get to hang out with great kids for a few hours after class. Aka, the easiest job ever.

♥

I'm exceedingly ambitious. I have lofty dreams ranging from being a best selling author to real estate tycoon to educational reform, and a lot more that I'm too shy to say. I love acting and writing and anything else where I can create and/or tell stories and I have every intention of putting those interests to use, too.

♥

I love learning and crave travel. My favorite place I've never been (and i've never been anywhere) is India, which if you've talked to me for more than 3 seconds I'm sure you know. I loves me some Bollywood especially (though no, that is not the extent of my knowledge or interest, thank you very much). I love learning new languages and I'm currently working on Hindi & Mandarin with a little Arabic & Korean on the side. I take pictures of absolutely everything, I've been vegetarian for 7 years & I am very much an ENFP and a libra.

♥

My life consists of a crazy family, a stinky boyfriend, goode friends, lovely (DZ) sisters, a gorgeous puppy and some cute stuffed animals, not to mention my green macbook pro & shiny DSLR (Digital Rebel XTi). Please point me back to this paragraph next time I forget that I have it pretty goode. =)
